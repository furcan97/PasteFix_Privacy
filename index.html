Privacy Policy and AI Processing Notice

Last updated: May 2, 2026

PasteFix AI helps users clean and rewrite copied text. This page explains what data is processed, why it is processed, and how users can contact us.

Short version: Instant text cleanup can run locally on your device. AI features may send the text you submit to our backend and Google Gemini for processing. We do not intend to permanently store raw user text on our servers.

1. Data We Process

  • Text you paste or type into the app for AI-powered actions.
  • Basic account information if you sign in, such as email or provider identifier.
  • Usage metadata such as action type, request time, app version, language, and usage counts.
  • Crash and diagnostic data needed to improve reliability.
  • Notification token if you enable result-ready notifications.
  • Advertising identifiers or ad interaction signals processed by Google AdMob when ads are shown.

2. Local and AI Processing

Instant tools such as spacing cleanup, emoji removal, link removal, line-break cleanup, and paragraph normalization are designed to run locally where possible.

AI tools may send submitted text to our backend. Our backend may send that text to Google Gemini to generate the result.

3. Storage and Retention

  • Local history is stored on your device by default.
  • Raw text is not intended to be permanently stored on our servers.
  • Temporary task data, if needed for processing, should be deleted when no longer necessary.
  • Usage metadata may be stored for limits, abuse prevention, analytics, and service quality.

4. Legal Basis

Personal data may be processed to provide the app, perform AI text actions requested by the user, maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve service quality.

When AI features are used, submitted text may be transferred to third-party infrastructure providers such as Google Gemini. Users should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal data unless necessary.

5. Notifications

If enabled, notifications are used only for useful product events such as telling you that an AI result is ready. Notification bodies should not include private user text.

6. Analytics

We may measure product health, such as most used tools, free limit events, AI latency, and crash reports. Raw input text, prompts, and AI outputs should not be sent to analytics.

7. Advertising

PasteFix AI may show Google AdMob banner ads and rewarded interstitial ads. Rewarded ads are optional and may be offered when an account reaches the free AI usage limit. Google AdMob may process device, ad, and interaction data according to Google's advertising and privacy policies.

8. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request account deletion and data deletion from inside the app or by contacting us. Local history can be cleared from the app settings.

9. Contact

For privacy, deletion, or support requests, contact: nacnakruf@gmail.com

10. Terms of Use

PasteFix AI is provided as a productivity tool for cleaning and improving text. Users are responsible for the text they submit and for reviewing generated results before use.

  • Do not use PasteFix AI to create illegal, abusive, deceptive, or harmful content.
  • Do not submit highly sensitive personal data unless necessary.
  • AI output may be imperfect. Review names, numbers, dates, links, and important facts before sharing.
  • The service may apply usage limits to protect reliability and cost.
